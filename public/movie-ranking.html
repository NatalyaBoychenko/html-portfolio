The Best Movies According to Natalia

My top 3 movies of all-time

1. The Avengers

The movie has a very good balance of heroism and humour, great characters' development, an interesting villain, great action-packed sequences and a big use of the CGI.


2. Sully

A complex, entertainning, nicely interpreted and surprisingly wonderful film that doesn't only carries the audience into a great untold story full of drama, where a tribute to an everyday hero is clearly saw.


3. The Simpsons

A funny, ground-breaking animated comedy with lightening-quick wit, insightful social and brilliantly integrated parody.
